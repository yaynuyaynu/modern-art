How this table works

On your turn, choose one painting from your hand. Its icon determines how that painting is auctioned.

When the fifth painting by any artist reaches the market, the round ends immediately. The three most fashionable artists pay $30, $20, and $10 per painting. Their values accumulate over four rounds.
